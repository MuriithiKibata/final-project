Welcome to Riara School of Computing

About School of Computing Sciences

The School of Computing Sciences is a dynamic hub for innovation, research, and academic excellence in the field of computer science and technology. We offer a broad range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with both foundational knowledge and cutting-edge skills in areas such as software engineering, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and systems development.

Programs We Offer

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Gain a strong foundation in computing with specialization options in software engineering, AI, and networks.

Diploma in Information Technology

Hands-on skills in system support, networking, and web development, ideal for tech support careers.

Postgraduate Diploma in Data Science

Learn data wrangling, visualization, machine learning, and big data analytics with real-world datasets.
